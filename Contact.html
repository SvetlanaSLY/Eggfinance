FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about contacting us.

What is your typical response time?

expand_more

We typically respond to all inquiries within 1 business day. For urgent technical issues, our support team aims to respond within 4 hours during business hours (9:00 – 18:00 CET, Monday to Friday).

Can I schedule a demo before contacting sales?

expand_more

Absolutely! You can request a personalized demo directly through our contact form. Simply select "Product Information" as your inquiry type and mention that you'd like to schedule a demo in your message.

Do you offer support in languages other than English?

expand_more

Yes, our team speaks multiple languages including Italian, English, Spanish, and French. When you contact us, feel free to write in your preferred language and we'll match you with the appropriate specialist.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

expand_more

To help us assist you better, please include: your name and company, a brief description of your needs or challenges, your preferred contact method, and any specific questions you have. The more details you provide, the better we can tailor our response to your needs.

Is there a cost for initial consultation?

expand_more

No, our initial consultation is completely free. We believe in understanding your needs first before discussing any potential solutions or pricing. There's no obligation, and we're here to help you find the best fit for your business.
